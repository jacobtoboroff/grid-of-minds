About Grid of Minds

Grid of Minds is a free daily trivia puzzle game that challenges your knowledge of U.S. presidents. Each day, a new 3x3 grid is generated with clues based on presidential history, political roles, eras, and more.

Your goal? Match each square to the correct U.S. president using logic, memory, and deduction — with just 9 guesses. It's like Wordle meets history, crafted for trivia lovers, students, and casual gamers alike.

Who Created It?

This game was created by a college student passionate about both American history and creative gameplay. The idea was to make learning about presidents fun, accessible, and addictive. Grid of Minds aims to make history feel like a game worth playing every day.
